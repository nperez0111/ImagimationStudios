In Production@Imagimation

The magically creative storytelling and diversified visions of Hal Miles Imagimation Studios vast in-house talent is what makes up our winning formula for a successful production. Currently we have many feature films, television series, and shorts in various stages of production and the #1 element that we believe in creating for each is a great Story. Without a Story you have nothing.

The majority of our projects are completely animated productions. Others are live action based and laced with the various animation and visual effects techniques that we are internationally known for such as stop motion animation, cg animation, and traditional animation.

Day of the Eclipse: The ‘Day of the Eclipse’ is a live action science fiction feature film that is currently in pre production. The gallery to the left contains the original theatrical teaser trailer for the film.

Little Ball Lost: ‘Little Ball Lost’ is a CG animated short subject that is currently in pre production. Check out a few storyboard panels for the short in the gallery to the left.
